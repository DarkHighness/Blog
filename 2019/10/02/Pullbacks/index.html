拉回 Pullbacks

在范畴论Category Theory中，一个数学分支，拉回Pullback（也称为纤维积或笛卡尔方块）是由具有公共上域的两个态射 f:X→Zf:X \to Zf:X→Z 与 g:Y→Zg:Y \to Zg:Y→Z 组成的图表的极限。拉回经常写作

P=X×ZY.P=X\times _{Z}Y.P=X×Z​Y.

In category theory, a branch of mathematics, a pullback (also called a fiber product, fibre product, fibered product or Cartesian square) is the limit of a diagram consisting of two morphisms f:X→Zf:X \to Zf:X→Z and $ g:Y \to Z $ with a common codomain. The pullback is often written

P=X×ZY.P=X\times _{Z}Y.P=X×Z​Y.
